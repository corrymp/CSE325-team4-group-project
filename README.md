# Plan2Gather

**Plan2Gather** is scheduling tool that helps groups find the best time to meet — no account required to respond to an event.

---

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

You don't need an account to respond to an event, but creating one lets you **create and manage your own events**.

1. Click **Create Account** in the top right corner
2. Choose a username (up to 16 characters)
3. Enter a password (at least 6 characters)
4. Optionally add an email address
5. Click **Register**

> **Basic vs Full account:** If you register without an email you get a Basic account. Adding an email gives you a Full account.

---

## Creating an Event

1. Log in and go to your **Profile**
2. Click **Create New Event**
3. Fill in the event details:
   - **Event Name** — up to 48 characters
   - **Description** — optional, up to 256 characters
   - **Start Time** — the earliest possible time slot
   - **Duration** — how long the event will last
   - **Allow Guests** — toggle this off if you only want registered users to respond
4. Click **Create** — you'll get a link to share with your group

---

## Responding to an Event

Anyone with the event link can respond — no account needed if the organizer allows guests.

1. Open the event link
2. Enter your name
3. Click the time slots that work for you
4. Click **Submit**

If you're a registered user, log in first and your response will be saved to your account.

---

## Viewing Results

Once people have responded, the event page shows an overview of everyone's availability. The time slots where the most people are free are highlighted so it's easy to find the best time.

---

## Managing Your Events

From your **Profile** page you can:
- See all events you've created
- Edit event details
- Delete events

---

## Logging Out

Click your username in the top right corner, then click **Logout**.

---

## FAQ

**Do I need an account to respond to an event?**
No — as long as the organizer has allowed guests, you can respond with just a name.

**Can I edit my availability after submitting?**
Yes — open the event link again, enter the same name, and resubmit.

**Is there a limit to how many events I can create?**
Not currently.

**I forgot my password — what do I do?**
Password reset is not yet available. Contact the event organizer to have your response removed, then resubmit under a new name.
